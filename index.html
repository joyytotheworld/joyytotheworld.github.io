ENHYPEN is a K-POP group formed by formed by Belift Lab. In 2020, there was a survival show called "I-LAND". There were about 12 K-POP trainees who were chosen to be part of the show. The contestants were given missions to do. For example, they had to prepare a performance and perform it infront of judges after a few days of practice. The group who did the best performance will stay in I-Land, and the other group will go to the Ground. The contestants in I-Land have a chance of debuting, while the contestants in the Ground have to do a better performance than the contestants in I-Land during the next performance to go to I-Land and have a chance of debuting. Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Niki were the 7 contestants who were given the privilage of debuting. Their journey of being K-POP artists began in November 30, 2020. Since then, they've made about 9 albums.
